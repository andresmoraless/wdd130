Believing in Jesus Christ

"The Doctrine of Jesus Christ: Faith in Jesus Christ"

2 Nephi 25:23-29

"...The right way is to believe in Christ"

The first dimension of faith in Jesus Christ starts by believing in Him. It first implies recognizing His identity, which involves acknowledging Jesus as the Son of God and the Messiah. To recognize His identity also means to accept His role as Savior, believing that Jesus atoned for our sins and offers salvation.

1 / 3

"And we talk of Christ, we rejoice in Christ, we preach of Christ..."

Another element of believing in Jesus Christ is to acknowledge His teachings. It includes accepting the scripture's account of His life, death, and resurrection, and following His teachings.

2 / 3

"...We are made alive in Christ because of our faith"

Lastly, believing in Jesus Christ involves a personal relationship with Him. Seeking guidance, comfort, and transformation is essential to exercising faith in Him.

3 / 3
